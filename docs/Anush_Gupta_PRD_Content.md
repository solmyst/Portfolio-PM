## 1. Executive Summary

Most teams struggle to find Product Managers who can balance execution, technical understanding, growth thinking, and ownership simultaneously.

**The Solution (Anush):** A technically driven Product Manager optimized for 0→1 product execution, fast iteration cycles, ambiguity handling, cross-functional collaboration, and growth-focused product thinking.

The product is designed to convert unclear ideas into structured execution plans, scalable systems, and measurable business outcomes.

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Modern startups move fast, but many PMs:

- Depend heavily on engineering for technical clarity
- Lack execution ownership
- Optimize for documentation instead of shipping
- Fail to balance business goals with user experience

**What teams actually need** — PMs who can communicate clearly, move fast under uncertainty, understand technical systems, collaborate deeply with engineering, and think both strategically and operationally.
